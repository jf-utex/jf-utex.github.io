About me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer and am currently searching for a position that will allow me to sharpen my coding skills, work in an agile environment and produce a product I am invested in. While attending a Bootcamp for coding, I have been concurrently finishing my MA in Human Dimensions of Organizations at the University of Texas. Prior to that, I earned my BS in Zoology, a BA in Liberal Arts with a minor in Computer Science. I have spent almost 30 years in patient care with a focus on pediatrics. During this time, I learned that while patient management is widely researched and explained, leadership in healthcare begs for more exploration.

This has brought me to my studies at the University of Texas where I hope to translate my new understanding about organizations and the ethical, psychological and sociological way we treat our employees into a more productive and engaged workforce. While allied health is where I have honed my skills in human relations, I am making a shift into technology to allow further growth and learning potential. I love solving problems and helping others relate to change with a positive reflection on the future. I am excited to learn more and practice Agile Development as the communication aspect of this practice leads right into my wheelhouse.

In my spare time, when I'm not solving elusive web development puzzles, I enjoy time with my supportive husband and three dogs. Fun for me is to train and compete in canine agility.
